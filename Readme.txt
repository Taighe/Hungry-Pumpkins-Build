Hungry Pumpkins Proof of Concept Proto Type Build

Default Controls:
w, a, s, d = Move
spacebar = Jump
Left Mouse Click = Feed Pumpkin Fruit
Right Mouse Click = Eat Fruit

Notes:
-Watermelons that you currently have in your inventory will slow you down.

-Feeding the Pumpkin will increase the pumpkins size depending on the fruit/vegetable.
